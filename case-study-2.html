Higher ARPDAU × growing DAU gap = multiplier effect. A’s long-tail retention wins by $7,562.

The Bottom Line

Short-term campaign? Choose B. Its strong early retention generates more revenue in a 15-day window.

Long-term game? Choose A. Puzzle games are years-long products. A’s flat retention curve accumulates more users every day and benefits more from any monetization event. The DAU gap grows from 307 on Day 15 to thousands by Day 30.
